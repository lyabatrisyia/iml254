MY BACKGROUND



Hi there, I am Alya Batrisyia


I am currently pursuing in Diploma in Library Informatic at UiTM Merbok, Kedah. I have interest in the intersection of infomation science and technology, and i'm pasionate about how libraries adapt to digital age.


Throughout my academic journey, I embarked on venture that initially seemed unfamilliar but become enriching experiences. One such endeavor involved delving into cataloging projects at our librray, something I had never imagined myself doing before. How ever, fueled by curiosity and a desire to learn, I decided to give it a try.


As I immersed myself in cataloging rare manuscripts and organizing information, I discovered a newfound passion for curating knowledge and facilitating access to resources. Additionally, i found myself draw to organizing information literacy workshops, despite having little prior experiences in this area. It was a journey of growth and discovery, where each step challenged me to expand my horizons and embrace new opportunities.


When I'm not diving into the world of books and databases, you'll often find me playing online games or drawing to unleash my creativity. Life is all about balance, after all!

STORY

I was born in 2004 and raised by both of my lovely parents in Sarawak. Most of the time, I was taken cared by a sitter because my mother was a full-time worker while my brothers are already in their school year. My sitter was a kind person, she always been nice to me and care about me when I was sick and alone.
